The Laser Fleet News


Laser Fleet

Lasers are the largest single class at Tata Steel Sailing Club and have their own start in all series except for Saturdays. Racing is always very competitive with sailors ranging from current and past squad members to relative beginners.

Sailors may use radial rigs and sail with the standard fleet but without handicap. 4.7 and 8.1 rigs sail in the general handicap fleets. To try and keep costs down and encourage competitive racing the club has recently decided to allow sailors to use non-laser made sails and spars for all races except at the club regatta.
