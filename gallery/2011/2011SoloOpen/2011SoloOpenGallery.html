2011 Solo Welsh National Event Gallery

The 17th running of the Solo Welsh Championship was held on Saturday 30 April and Sunday 1 May 2011.

This gallery was put together by photographs taken by various members of the Tata Steel Sailing Club and edited by Paul Bambridge. The gallery was collated with the help of Bob Taylor.
