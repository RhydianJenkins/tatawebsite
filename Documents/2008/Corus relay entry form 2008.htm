Corus Relay Triathlon

Eglyws Nunydd Reservoir

Sunday 1th June 2008

Start 8.00am

This is a team event open to Corus employees and contractors where one member completes a 400m open water swim, a second member cycles 10 miles along the A48 to Pyle and back before handing over to the third member who will complete the race with a flat 3km run around the reservoir.

Team Entry Fee  £30.00. Excluding costs of day licence insurance and some set up costs, a majority of this money will be donated to charity.
