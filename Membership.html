Club Membership

The Club welcomes experienced sailors and those wanting to give sailing a try, young and old. Membership is open to Tata Steel employees and members of the general public. The rates for Tata Steel and non Tata Steel membership (associate membership) is slightly different. Please select the appropriate button to display the membership forms and fees for each type of membership. Note that membership fees include a compound space for your dinghy or windsurfer storage.

The documents are available in pdf or Browser format for you to view and print off. It would be helpful to the membership secretary if the forms can be printed double sided. The forms are applicable to membership starting from 1st January. 

Tata Steel Sailing Club is again offering the Christmas Deal. It means that if anyone wants to join early, they can. All they have to do is pay their year's membership fees in December and then enjoy their sailing at the club through to the end of the year with no additional charge.
