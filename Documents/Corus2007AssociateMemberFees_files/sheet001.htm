Safety Note
Protruding stakes may lead to a claim against you for personal injury.  Boats must be secured via two side
stakes each at least 0.8 metres long hammered completely below ground with only rope tails protruding;
craft with masts stepped must also have bows secured. Boat trailers must be taken home or kept kept
under your dinghy. To keep the club tidy, and reduce the risk of fire in dry months,  it is your duty to keep
the grass cut short in your compound area.
